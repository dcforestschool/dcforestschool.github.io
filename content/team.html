Our Team



Sara

Sara is a teacher, forest school leader, tutor, mum and the founder of Dainton Copse Forest School.



She was lucky to have an ‘outdoorsy’ childhood and loves to share her passion for the outdoors with the visitors that come to the woodland.



Knowing that our children are the future caretakers of our planet motivates her to support young people in fostering positive relationships with our wonderful natural world.



Sara is also a big fan of PLAY! She loves how our beautiful woodland provides countless opportunities for discovery, fun, creativity and problem-solving. Interacting with the natural environment allows children to learn by doing and experiment with ideas; perfect for developing inquisitive minds!



Emma

Emma is a teacher and mum who discovered a real passion for the outdoors when taking part in Forest School sessions with her young daughter.



She has seen and experienced how the outdoor environment really helps engage and stimulate young children as well as provide a calming, free space for all to enjoy, regardless of age or limitation.



As an experienced and passionate Early Years practitioner, Emma is especially committed to helping young learners develop and thrive. She believes that Forest School provides the perfect environment for the development of inquisitive, resilient learners who love and want to protect the world around them.



Emma met Sara whilst volunteering at another Forest School. She was delighted to be invited to help set up Dainton Copse Forest School based on a shared vision of opening up this wonderful outdoor space to the local community to be respectfully enjoyed by all who visit.
